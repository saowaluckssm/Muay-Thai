Classes

Muay Thai (Thai: มวยไทย, Muay thai, pronounced [mūa̯j tʰāj] sometimes referred to as "Thai boxing", is a martial art and combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "art of eight limbs" as it is characterised by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees and shins. This martial art is practical for self defense and health and fitness.

Fighters training

is two hour long and includes, pad work , mitt work, sparring , clinch and bag work. There are two sessions offered a day. Included is a private weight room, showers, and locker rooms. Fighters will be printed on social media and will be matched for amateur and professional competitions on a monthly basis. Fighters are required to get their own blood work, physicals and fighters licenses. Transportation to fights are provided. The cost is $150 a month

Beginner Program

Basic footwork, punches, kicks, elbows, knees and clinch are taught to the student. Practice is done with an instructor on the open floor and bags utilizing offensive and defensive techniques. This course includes a warm up and basic exercises to train the body and get into shape. This course is 3 months long. The cost is $125.00 a month

Teens Muay Thai

is a program design to train your Teen for self -defense, health and fitness and competition. Competition is at the trainers and parents discretion. Teens develop self confidence, patience, respect for themselves and others. Ages 11-14. This is a six month course. The cost is $125.00 a month

Kids Muay Thai

is a program design to train your Teen for self -defense, health and fitness and competition. Sparring is at the trainers and parents discretion. Kids develop self confidence, patience, respect for themselves and others. Ages 6-10. This is a six month course. The cost is $99.00 a month

Little Kids Muay Thai

is a program design to train your Teen for self -defense, health and fitness and agility . Little kids develop self confidence, patience, respect for themselves and others by doing a specific training course designed to teach agility, flexibility and martial arts basics. Ages 3-5 This is a two month course. The cost is $99.95 a month
